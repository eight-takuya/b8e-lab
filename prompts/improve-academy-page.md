## Context

The Academy page is the most intimate page on the site.
It speaks to someone already in the process of inner questioning.

Its core intention: this is not a course catalog. It is a space the reader can return to themselves.

Key concepts to hold:
- **Dreamin' Spiral Academy** — founded 2025, born from 28 years of realizing external change isn't enough
- **Dreamin' Engine** — a framework of four elements: Consciousness, Body, Action, Future Vision. Not a system — an alignment approach.
- **Spiral growth** — not linear progress. The same questions, seen from a deeper place each time.
- **Human presence** — the founder is in the room, not as a teacher, but as a fellow traveler.

The page should feel like arriving somewhere quiet. Not being enrolled in something.
