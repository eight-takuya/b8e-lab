## Context

The DX page communicates B8E's approach to digital transformation.
Its core intention: DX is not a technical problem — it is an alignment question.
B8E's role is not to solve but to walk alongside.

The DX page must:
- Avoid standard consulting language
- Connect external (organizational) transformation to internal (human) state
- Show that the conversation can begin before the client has a clear problem
- Feel like a grounded, experienced voice — not a pitch
