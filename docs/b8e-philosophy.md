## Core Principles

### Being over Doing
Lead with existence and presence, not action or output.
The site does not introduce what B8E does. It shows what B8E is.

### Resonance over Persuasion
The reader should feel "this is for me" — without being told.
No claims, no urgency, no stacked proof.

### Natural Flow over Forced Structure
Each section exists because it creates meaning, not because a template demands it.
Silence and space are intentional.

### Alignment → Flow → Creation
Internal alignment precedes external action.
The site reflects this: philosophy first, services second, contact last.

### Truth Is Felt, Not Imposed
Do not tell the reader what to think.
Create space for them to feel it themselves.

## Communication Style

- Calm, grounded, clear
- No exaggeration or hype
- No consulting jargon (avoid: "ソリューション", "バリュー", "デリバー")
- Speak to the reader's inner awareness, not their decision-making logic
- Use questions more than declarations
- Short sentences. White space between thoughts.

---

## Website Philosophy

The B8E website is not for selling. It is for resonance.

A visitor arrives, reads, and either feels "this is mine" — or moves on.
Both outcomes are acceptable. Pressure is not.

Every design decision should answer:
> "Does this create space, or does it close it?"
